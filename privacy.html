Your Privacy Matters

Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2025

1. No Data Collection

PDF Master Suite Pro runs entirely in your browser. We do not operate any servers that receive your files. No PDFs, images, or personal data are ever uploaded or stored by us.

2. Local Processing Only

All PDF operations (merge, split, conversion, password, OCR, audio, etc.) happen on your device. Your data never leaves your computer.

3. Optional Account (Local Storage)

If you choose to create an account, your name and email are stored only in your browser's localStorage. No backend database exists. You can delete this data at any time by clearing your browser's local storage.

4. Camera & Microphone Access

Optional features (Scan to PDF, Audio MP3 recording) request camera or microphone access. These permissions are used only for the active feature and only while you use it. Images and audio are never transmitted to any server; they are processed locally and discarded after use.

5. No Tracking

We do not use cookies, analytics, or any tracking technologies. We have no interest in monitoring your activity.

6. Third-Party Libraries

The Tool uses open-source libraries that may run locally. Those libraries do not send data to external servers either.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Tool does not knowingly collect any personal information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page.

9. Contact Us

If you have privacy concerns, please contact us via the GitHub repository.

Your trust is important to us. We built this Tool with privacy as the #1 priority. No servers, no tracking, no data collection — just pure client-side functionality.
